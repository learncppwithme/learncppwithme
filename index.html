overview
i'm learning c++ because of competitive programming, more specifically competing in the USACO contests, or United States of America Computing Olympiad. these are online programming contests that take place in the winter every year, and has four levels: bronze, silver, gold, and platinum. if you score high enough in platinum you can potentially be invited to the USACO summer training program, where they pick the top four participants to represent the USA in the International Olympiad in Informatics (IOI), they also pick the top four girls to represent the USA in the European Girls Olympiad in Informatics (EGOI). I'm not really looking to get to this level, my goal is to get to the gold division! i'm also not really a competitive person at all (kind of ironic because it's called "competitive" programming", so i'm just trying to have fun and improve my programming/problem solving skills! and learn a new language while i do it!
